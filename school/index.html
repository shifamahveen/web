Govt. High School

The school is a government institution offering a comprehensive educational program from grades 1 to 10. Established in 1953, it is a fully English-medium school. The campus accommodates both a primary school (grades 1 to 5) and a high school (grades 6 to 10), making it a vital center for education in the local community.

The school also serves as an open school center for grades 10 and 12 under the Telangana Open School Society (TOSS), enabling students to complete their education flexibly.

The school campus also includes office of Deputy E.O, Golconda Zone.

About Us

The school's administration is overseen by a Deputy Education Officer, ensuring compliance with educational policies and standards. There is strong support from parents, who are actively involved in the school's progress and its students' academic success.

Key Features:

  1. NCC Unit: The school is the only government high school in its area to offer an NCC (National Cadet Corps) unit, providing students with the opportunity for disciplined, physical training alongside academic education.
  2. Open School Center: The school also serves as an open school center for grades 10 and 12 under the Telangana Open School Society (TOSS), enabling students to complete their education flexibly.
  3. Midday Meal Scheme & Free Uniforms: The institution supports the governmentâ€™s midday meal scheme, ensuring all students receive nutritious meals. It also provides free textbooks and uniforms to its students.
  4. Active Participation in Government Initiatives: The school participates in various government initiatives, including the "Swachh Bharat" (Clean India) campaign, where departments work together to promote cleanliness and hygiene.
  5. Atal Tinkering Lab: The school provides ATL which is an activity based learning in the identified schools across the country where young minds can give shape to their ideas for societal problems.

NCC Facility

Our school offers an exclusive NCC facility where military personnel provide professional training to the students. The training focuses on physical endurance, discipline, and leadership development.

Students who perform exceptionally well are given the opportunity to receive advanced training at the prestigious Secundarabad Military Center, offering them a pathway to outstanding career opportunities in the defense sector.
